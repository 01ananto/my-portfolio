Dear Member,


We’re thrilled to officially launch our new and improved roadside assistance program through Liberty Lane Roadside!

Liberty Lane Roadside was developed around feedback provided by AMAC members. With a focus on offering simpler plan options, an expanded roadside assistance network, and helpful new enhancements, we strived to ensure that your experience with our new roadside assistance benefit is best-in-class - all while maintaining competitive pricing!

  
 
 

Why Liberty Lane?

Liberty Lane Roadside Assistance plan holders have access to a nationwide network of over 35,000 roadside assistance providers - 24 hours per day, 7 days per week, 365 days per year - no matter where you are in the U.S. You can count on our dedicated, U.S.-based team of dispatch professionals to be your trusted partner on the road.

Comprehensive services to get you back on the road safely:

Towing Services

Breakdown? We’ll tow your vehicle to the nearest service center or your chosen destination.
Flat Tire Change

Flat tire? No problem. Our roadside team will replace it with your spare and get you rolling again in no time.
Auto Lock Out

Locked out? Our technicians can help you regain access to your vehicle quickly and securely.
Battery Jump Start

Dead battery? We’ll jump-start your car on the spot so you can get back on the road without the wait.
Fuel Delivery

Out of gas? We’ll deliver fuel directly to your location, so you’re never stuck on empty.
Winching Service

Stuck in mud, snow, or a ditch? Our winch service will pull your vehicle back to safety.
