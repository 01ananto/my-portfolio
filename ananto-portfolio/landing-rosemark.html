Getting to Know RoseMark

RoseMark Advisors is the exclusive provider of financial services for AMAC members. For years, RoseMark has helped thousands of members build long-term strategies to create stronger financial futures. Our advisors will use their industry expertise to provide you a smart solution while safeguarding the things most important to you. RoseMark is a New York State registered investment advisory firm with a fiduciary duty to provide the best information, advice, services, and products to AMAC members.

Our holistic approach to planning allows us to get a clear picture of your current financial situation and helps us set your path to the future. Our advisors offer a FREE cashflow analysis of assets and liabilities to prepare for your retirement. When you work with RoseMark, you receive the benefit of individualized service and timely education that founder John Wesley envisioned.
